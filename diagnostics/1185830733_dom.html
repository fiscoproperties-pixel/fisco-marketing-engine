Property Description

Million-Dollar Views Meet Semi-Custom Luxury in Coveted Catalina Bay! Welcome to Catalina Bay, one of Saratoga Springs' most sought-after communities, where breathtaking views of Utah Lake and the Majestic Wasatch Mountains create a lifestyle unlike any other. Perfectly situated on a peaceful cul-de-sac, this exceptional semi-custom home offers the ideal blend of luxury, comfort, and outdoor adventure. From the moment you enter, you'll be captivated by the abundance of natural light that fills the home, highlighting the open-concept design and stunning finishes throughout. The heart of the home is a true Chef's Kitchen, showcasing premium stainless-steel appliances, a gas range, double ovens, custom wood cabinetry, a handcrafted wood hood, and exquisite quartz countertops complemented by striking Leather Granite surfaces-creating a sophisticated space designed for gathering, entertaining, and culinary excellence. Thoughtful upgrades extend throughout the home, including high-end custom wood closet organizers in every closet, providing both elegance and functionality. The spacious, fully finished walkout basement offers additional living and entertaining space while providing direct access to your private backyard retreat complete with a cozy fire pit-perfect for relaxing evenings under the stars. Designed with outdoor enthusiasts and adventurers in mind, this property boasts an impressive 12' x 12' RV garage that doubles as a deep tandem fourth-car garage, providing exceptional flexibility and storage. The expansive RV parking pad features a dedicated 50-amp hookup, while the RV garage includes its own 30-amp hookup, making it easy to accommodate your RV, recreational vehicles, or workshop needs. These extensive upgrades add tremendous value and convenience, setting this home apart from others and offering capabilities rarely found in today's market. Living in Catalina Bay means enjoying the very best of Utah's outdoor lifestyle. Just minutes away, you'll find Talons Cove Golf Course, Utah Lake, scenic hiking and biking trails, off-road recreation areas, shooting ranges, camping destinations, and a brand-new grocery store currently under construction. In addition, you'll be less than 15 minutes from premier shopping centers, retail destinations, dining establishments, and everyday conveniences-offering a lifestyle that seamlessly meets all your needs while maintaining the peace and serenity of this exceptional community. With stunning million-dollar views, premium upgrades, abundant natural light, incredible RV accommodations, and an unbeatable location, this remarkable Catalina Bay home offers a rare opportunity to enjoy luxury living surrounded by some of Utah's most spectacular scenery.
